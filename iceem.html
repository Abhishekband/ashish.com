About College

ICEEM-International Centre of Excellence in Engineering
and Management Chh. Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad) was
established in 2011, under the leadership of our Founder-Chairman,
Shri. Bhujangrao Kulkarni, Retd. IAS. The institute aims at
providing Quality Education in Engineering & Management, that
nourishes the upcoming Engineers, Entrepreneurs and Managers.
bICEEM is Aprroved by AICTE, New Delhi Recognized by DTE,
(Govt, of Maharashtra) and Affiliated to
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Chh. Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad).
ICEEM has made steady progress in last Twelve
years towards its vision of becoming one of the hundred
best "Technical Campuses" in India by 2025.
